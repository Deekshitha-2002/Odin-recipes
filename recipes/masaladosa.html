Crispy Masala dosa!


The classic Masala dosa recipe makes perfectly light, soft and crispy crepes stuffed with a savory, wonderfully spiced potato and onion filling!

Ingredients required:

  • 3 cups Dosa Batter
  • 4 to 5 medium Potatoes, boiled (approx. 2 cups when chopped)
  • 1 large Onion, thinly sliced (approx. 1/2 cup)
  • 1/2 teaspoon Mustard Seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon Cumin Seeds
  • 1 teaspoon Chana Dal (Bengal gram), soaked in hot water for 30 minutes
  • A pinch of Asafoetida (hing)
  • 8-10 Curry leaves
  • 1 Green Chilli, finely chopped (or to taste)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2-3 tablespoons finely chopped Coriander Leaves
  • 2-3 tablespoons Butter or Oil, for greasing while making dosa

The process!

  1. Use ready made dosa batter.
  2. Boil potatoes in 2½-3 cups water until potatoes turn soft.
  3. Heat oil in a kadai and mustard seeds. When they begin to crackle, add asafoetida, urad dal, chana dal and cumin seeds.
  4. Sauté until dal turns light brown.
  5. Add finely chopped onion, turmeric powder and salt.
  6. Let it cook over medium flame.
  7. Add chopped potatoes.
  8. Mix well and lightly mash them. Cook until mixture turns thick, for around 4-5 minutes.
  9. Add finely chopped coriander leaves and mix well. Stuffing for masala dosa is ready!
  10. Heat non-stick tawa. When it is medium hot, sprinkle few drops of water over the surface and wait until water evaporates.
  11. Place a ladle full batter in the center and spread it thin and evenly by moving ladle in clockwise or anti clockwise direction.
  12. Place 3-tablespoons potato stuffing (masala) in the middle and press it gently using a flat spoon.
  13. Cook dosa until bottom surface turns light golden brown and crisp
  14. Transfer it to a serving plate.
  15. Serve with Sambar and coconut chutney!
