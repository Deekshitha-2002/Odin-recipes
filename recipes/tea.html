Authentic Masala Chai!


There's nothing as good as the perfect cup of Indian Masala Chai whether it's in the middle of summer or for cozying up in the winter.It is simmered with warming and fragrant spices and is best served with Parle-G biscuits or cake rusk for dipping.

Ingredients required:

  • Black tea powder
  • Water
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Spices and herbs

The process!

  1. Add 1 inch cinnamon, 2 to 3 green cardamoms, 2 cloves and 1-inch peeled ginger to a mortar-pestle.
  2. Crush coarsely with the pestle and set aside.
  3. Using a saucepan or small pan with a handle, heat 2 cups of water (500 ml).
  4. Let the water come to a boil and add the crushed spices.
  5. Boil for 2 to 3 minutes. Add sugar to taste.
  6. Add 4 teaspoons of raw sugar.
  7. Now add 2 teaspoons of Assam tea powder or tea dust. Boil for 1 minute.
  8. Add Â¼ cup of milk
  9. After adding milk boil for 2 to 3 minutes.
  10. Pour the masala tea through a tea strainer directly into a cup.
  11. Serve Masala chai steaming hot with Indian snacks of your choice such as aloo pakora, veg cutlet, samosa, aloo tikki, onion pakoda, bread pakora, medu vada, biscuits or cookies.
