Chicken fry!


This is crazy-crisp, spicy and super good! Yes, the Chicken Fry that you will never be bored of. The juicy chicken pieces blanketed in a mix of basic spices, then deep fried to crisp outer and succulent within.

Ingredients required:

  • 1 Kg Chicken (cut into 8 pieces)
  • To deep fry Oil
  • Mix together for marinade:
  • 1/2 tsp Ginger paste
  • Salt
  • 2 tsp Chilli powder
  • 2 tbsp Gram flour and 2 tbsp Vinegar
  • Readymade chicken masala powder
  • 1/2 cup Mustard oil
  • Lemon twists and coriander

The process!

  1. Prick the chicken in 2-3 places, marinate in the mixture and keep for 3-4 hrs.
  2. Heat the oil in a heavy-based pan and put the chicken pieces into it.
  3. Deep-fry over a medium flame, till the chicken is tender.
  4. It takes 15-20 minutes.
  5. Serve garnished with lemon twists and coriander leaves.
